1. Home exercises: mafeesh gheer el hand grip el ana eshtaret-ha de. Tool manta a3ed fel beet, fel office, 5areg 
fi ay 7eta e3mel beha. Hate3mel beha eh? Fih no3een men el tamareen beha:
	a. Enak te2fel 3al grip le modet 6-7 seconds w teqarar el tamreen da 10 3adat fi kol 2eed.
	b. Enk tefdal 2afel atwal wa2t momken mara fi kol 2eed.
Fa o3od switch mabeen el tamreneen dool tool manta a3ed fady.
